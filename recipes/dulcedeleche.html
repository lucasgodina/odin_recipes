Dulce de Leche

Ingredients

  • 1 can of sweetened condensed milk
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Remove the label from the can of sweetened condensed milk.
  2. Place the unopened can in a large pot.
  3. Add enough water to the pot to completely submerge the can.
  4. Bring the water to a boil over medium-high heat.
  5. Reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for 2-3 hours.
  6. Make sure to check the water level periodically and add more water if needed to keep the can fully submerged.
  7. After 2-3 hours, carefully remove the can from the pot and let it cool completely before opening.
  8. Once cooled, open the can to reveal the delicious dulce de leche!

Serving Suggestions

Dulce de leche can be enjoyed in various ways:

  • Spread it on toast or pancakes.
  • Use it as a filling for cakes, cookies, or pastries.
  • Drizzle it over ice cream or fresh fruit.
  • Enjoy it straight from the spoon!

Enjoy your homemade dulce de leche!
