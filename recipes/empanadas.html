Argentinian Beef Empanadas

Ingredients:

  • Empanada dough
  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Spices (e.g., cumin, paprika, salt, pepper)
  • Vegetables (e.g., bell peppers, green olives)
  • Hard-boiled eggs
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the filling by saut√©ing the onion and garlic in oil.
  2. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  3. Season with spices and add the vegetables and chopped hard-boiled eggs. Cook until tender.
  4. Roll out the empanada dough and cut into circles.
  5. Place a spoonful of the filling in the center of each dough circle.
  6. Fold the dough over the filling and seal the edges by pressing with a fork.
  7. Heat oil in a pan and fry the empanadas until golden brown.
  8. Remove from the pan and drain on paper towels.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy!
