Argentinian Locro

Ingredients:

  • 500g white corn kernels
  • 500g pork meat, diced
  • 500g beef meat, diced
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Water

Instructions:

  1. Soak the white corn kernels in water overnight.
  2. In a large pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat.
  3. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and saut√© until golden brown.
  4. Add the diced pork and beef, and cook until browned.
  5. Drain the soaked corn kernels and add them to the pot.
  6. Add enough water to cover the ingredients, and bring to a boil.
  7. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and simmer for about 2 hours or until the corn kernels are tender.
  8. Add the paprika, ground cumin, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir well.
  9. Continue simmering for another 30 minutes.
  10. Remove from heat and let it rest for a few minutes before serving.

Enjoy your delicious Argentinian Locro!
